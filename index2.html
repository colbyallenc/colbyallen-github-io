who am i?

I am thrilled and driven by innovation. I am experienced with all stages of the development cycle for dynamic web projects. A combined knowledge of software engineering, psychology, and design allows me to be creative, innovative, and imaginative all while coming up with ways to make for the best user experience. Thinking and creating in ways that will make for a better experience for my community is the purpose I intend to serve. Possessing the skills to turn what may seem like an intangible idea into a tangible application intrigues me. As I continue to learn programming, I understand that problem solving is my driving force. I find joy in creating.
